Hi, I'm Nik!

I'm a software developer with a focus on iOS applications and a passion for beautiful useable user interface design.

Portfolio

Below is a list of applications available in the App Store for which I lead the design and development.

ALGO Traffic

An iOS app informing people in Alabama about current road conditions on roads managed by the Alabama Department of Transportation.

UA Safety

An iOS app for students, faculty, and anybody who would like to receive safety related information for the University of Alabama campus area such as weather alerts or general campus safety alerts.

Tuscaloosa County Sheriff

An iOS for the Tuscaloosa County Sheriff Office for safety related resources such as recent arrests, sex offender registries, tornado shelter locations,  and more.

Technologies

Below is a list of technologies and languages I am familiar with and use during the development life cycle of the apps I work on.

Swift

Swift is my language of choice. I use Swift for everything I can, which includes app development as well as server-side development.

Xcode

Xcode is my IDE of choice. I use Xcode for all of my main development tasks.

Sketch

I use Sketch to design and mockup app projects. Sketch allows me to iterate on design ideas quickly and lets me easily share mockups or assets with team members.

Vapor

I have started learning Vapor in order to write services that facilitate the communication between apps and databases. I love the ability to use Swift both in front-end and server-side development.

PostgreSQL

PostgreSQL is my database of choice to use in conjunction with Vapor as it is a powerful database that is also easy to interact with.
